Elevator Pitch

Our goal is to create a platform for donors to donate to charitable causes. The website will show potential donors various campaigns that they might be interested in donating to. specifically helping young children and mothers. We make it easy to donate, don't worry about not knowing which campaign to donate to, we'll show you the most popular choices! Why Donate? We believe that it's our duty to share our good fortune with others less fortunate, if it's $1 or $1000 it's the same. We value all human life and see it as our duty to facilitate this charitable business.

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website will be to collect donations for worthy causes. We will be a nonprofit organization to help raise money for less fortunate families and especially children. We will be collecting all the money and redistributing it to the correct organizations as per the will of the donator. We will display a list of possible organizations to donate to and will let the user input a message or short text option. Then we will also collect and process the donator's payment directly on our site and then at certain intervals, we will disperse it according to the user's selections.

Audience Statement

The target audience is mostly older people who have extra money. Maybe retired or widowed people. People who feel like they are missing some part of life and desire to do good, and to feel good, by making monetary donations for good causes. They would want to be able to easily make donations and be able to see the difference they are making. This could be accomplished by creating an email list and newsletter. They need to be able to feel like they are doing a good thing making this donation. This is what we are here for, to be a place to facilitate this need.

Misson Statement

Our mission is to bring relief and happiness to people via the means of monetary donations made by our donors. Having been blessed to find ourselves in good standing, we desire to help those who are less fortunate and need help. Our priority is helping young children while in their valuable impressionable youth to get the greatest head start in life possible. We want to be that miracle that has been prayed for. We operate on the terms that all human life is valuable and worthwhile in the sight of God. We simply wish to share our abilities and good fortune with those less fortunate.
